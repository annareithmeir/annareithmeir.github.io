Past Projects

  • Android Application using AR

    I developed a small Android application for an augmented art gallery experience. When the back-camera is used the app augments real-world artwork while the front-camera interacts with the user's face by enhancing his or her face with selected pieces ao art. I developed the app in order to try out AR development in Unity.
    --> C# Android Unity Vuforia

  • Predicting secondary structure of Proteins with Neural Networks and Multitask learning

    Neural networks have proven to be useful for the prediction of the3D protein structure. This is usually done by representing the amino acid sequences as multiple sequence alignments (MSA). However, the complexity of computing such MSAs poses a challenge which is why a method which does not rely on the computation of MSAs is desirable. In the scope of my Bachelor thesis, I investigated to what extent the use of NLP methods such as word embeddings and LSTMs can improve the performance of deep neural networks in protein structure prediction without the use of MSAs. Moreover, I investigated if multi-task learning of additional structural features further improve the predictions.
    --> PyTorch LSTMs NLP Multitask Learning
