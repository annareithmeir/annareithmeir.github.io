Past Projects

Prediction of price forward curves in the energy market

11/20    

In my IDP at the Center for Energy Markets I develop a model to predict price forward curves of the electricity price with a Linear Program.

Android augmented art

11/19    

I developed a small Android application for an augmented art gallery experience. When the back-camera is used the app augments realworld artwork while the front-camera interacts with the user's face by enhancing his or her face with selected pieces of art. This was a personal project for myself to try out AR development in Unity.

Predicting secondary structure of proteins with neural networks and multitask learning

04/19    

In the scope of my Bachelor's thesis I investigated to what extent the use of natural language processing methods such as word embeddings and LSTMs can improve the performance of deep neural networks in protein structure prediction. In addition to the structure further features were learned with the help of multi-task learning.

Code plagiarism checking tool

08/19    

This tool detects code plagiarisms in student coding homework submitted to the ARTEMIS platform. The results are shown graphically in the form of similarity graphs.

Porting OctNet to PyTorch

08/19    

I worked on porting the OctNet code from Lua to Python to make it usable with PyTorch.

Image gallery in VR with Leap Motion

08/19    

I developed an image gallery in VR which can be used with the LeapMotion for direct interaction with hands and gestures.

3D image reconstruction from CT scans

02/18    

Yet another uni project. Here I used C++ and Qt to reconstruct images from tomography scans in dark-field imaging. This was my first C++ project and got me hooked on this language.
